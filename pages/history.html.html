Barangay Bulabud

The name PICASO appeared during the Spanish period. This area later became four barangays. These are Bulabud, Rosario, Sugnod, and Sipac. Three separated for different reasons. Barrio Bulabud remained a village under the former municipality of Banga.

The name Bulabud, based on elders accounts, also came from the Spanish era. The name came from the river called Bulabud River. When it floods, the water twists and spreads in many directions. This condition still exists today.

The original settler of Barangay Bulabud, based on the research of Leonardo B. Ortega, former Teniente del Barrio, was Hues Pablo Rose, also known as Amboy Rose. He reportedly owned about sixty percent of the land in Bulabud. His heirs still own this land today.

The first leaders were Teniente Simplicio Ramos, Teniente Jose Rubio, and Teniente Potenciano Retome. This occurred during World War II. They appointed Sub-Tenientes in the sitios of Barrio Bulabud. These were Sitio Bati-ano, Minuro, Naeangae, Centro, and Ilaya.

Teniente Bienvenido Mirano succeeded Potenciano Inisa. During his term, a chapel was built in the barrio. The patron saint is Señor San Isidro Labrador. The feast continues every year on May fourteen and fifteen.

Teniente Proceso Inisa succeeded next. During his term, the grade school in Bulabud started. Classes were held in private houses. Later, the establishment of Bulabud Elementary School was approved. His family donated the land for the school.

Recardo Renacido succeeded him but served for a short time due to illness. He later passed away. Leadership returned to Teniente Bienvenido Mirano.

Teniente Leonardo Ortega later assumed office. During his term, the first road in Barangay Bulabud was opened. This was done through a council resolution submitted to former official Pastrana and forwarded to Secretary Juan Pajo. The project was approved and four thousand pesos were released. Teniente Leonardo Ortega supervised the construction until completion.

A barangay election was held and Clarito Renacido won as Barangay Captain. During his term, the barangay high school opened. In nineteen seventy-nine, an irrigation system was built, increasing farm production. In nineteen eighty-three, passenger motorcycles began operating. In nineteen eighty-five, jeep services started from Bulabud to Kalibo.

Captain Clarito Renacido was succeeded by his wife, Captain Nelly Renacido. During her term, a self-help multipurpose pavement was built in the center of Bulabud. After one term, Captain Pepito Maagma assumed office.

Ro ngaeang PICASO hay nagguwa ku panyempo it Kastila. Raya nga lugar hay nagin ap-at nga barangay: ro Bulabud, Rosario, Sugnod, ag Sipac. Tatlo ro naghiwalay sa sari-sari nga rason. Ro Barrio Bulabud hay nagpabilin nga sangka baryo sa idaeom it banwa it Banga.

Ro ngaeang Bulabud, base sa mga istorya it kamagueangan, hay naghalin man ku panyempo it Kastila. Raya hay naghalin sa suba nga ginatawag nga Bulabud River. Kon naga-baha, ro tubi hay nagaliso-liso ag nagakalat sa sari-sari nga direksyon.

Ro pinaka-unang nag-istar sa Barangay Bulabud hay si Hues Pablo Rose, nga kilaea man sa ngaeang Amboy Rose. Suno sa istorya, ana ro sesenta porsyento it euta sa Bulabud. Ro anang mga manunubli hay tag-iya gihapon it euta hasta sa makaron.

Ro mga unang naging pinuno hay sanday Teniente Simplicio Ramos, Teniente Jose Rubio, ag Teniente Potenciano Retome ku panyempo it Ikaduwa nga Gubat Pangkalibutan.

Si Teniente Bienvenido Mirano ro nagbueos kay Potenciano Inisa. Sa anang panyempo, gin-obra ro kapilya sa baryo. Ro patron saint hay si Señor San Isidro Labrador. Ro kapiestahan hay ginaselebrar kada Mayo katorse ag kince.

Nagbueos si Teniente Proceso Inisa. Sa anang panyempo, nag-umpisa ro grade school sa Bulabud. Dayon, na-aprubahan ro pagpundar it Bulabud Elementary School. Ro anang pamilya ro nag-donar it euta para sa eskwelahan.

Sinundan imaw ni Recardo Renacido pero madali eang ro anang serbisyo bangud sa masakit hasta nga imaw hay napatay. Bumalik ro pagka-pinuno kay Teniente Bienvenido Mirano.

Nag-asumir dayon si Teniente Leonardo Ortega. Sa anang panyempo, gin-ubra ro pinaka-unang kalsada sa Barangay Bulabud. Na-aprubahan ro proyekto ag nagpagguwa it ap-at ka libo nga piso. Gin-dumaeahan ni Teniente Ortega ro construction hasta nga natapos.

Nagka-eleksyon ag si Clarito Renacido ro nagdaog bilang Barangay Captain. Sa anang panyempo, nag-umpisa ro barangay high school. Ku 1979, gin-obra ro irrigation system. Ku 1983, nag-umpisa ro mga motor nga naga-pasahero, ag ku 1985 hay nag-umpisa ro mga jeep halin sa Bulabud paadto sa Kalibo.

Si Captain Clarito Renacido hay gin-bueusan it anang asawa nga si Captain Nelly Renacido. Sa anang panyempo, gin-obra ro multipurpose pavement sa centro it Bulabud.

Narrated by: Leonardo B. Ortega (Former Brgy. Captain)
